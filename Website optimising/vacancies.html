Front-End Developer

Requirements:

  • Proficiency in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
  • Experience with front-end frameworks such as React, Angular, or Vue.js.
  • Strong understanding of responsive and adaptive design principles.
  • Familiarity with version control systems like Git.
  • Knowledge of cross-browser compatibility and accessibility standards.
  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or a related field (or equivalent experience).
  • 2+ years of front-end development experience.

Responsibilities:

  • Translate UI/UX design mockups into interactive, functional web pages.
  • Optimize web pages for speed, scalability, and SEO best practices.
  • Ensure cross-browser and cross-device compatibility.
  • Collaborate with back-end developers to integrate APIs and services.
  • Maintain and improve existing web applications by identifying and fixing bugs.

Back-End Developer

Requirements:

  • Proficiency in server-side programming languages (e.g., Node.js, Python, Java).
  • Experience with relational and NoSQL databases (MySQL, PostgreSQL, MongoDB).
  • Familiarity with RESTful API design and microservices architecture.
  • Knowledge of authentication, authorization, and security best practices.
  • Version control experience (Git).
  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or related field (or equivalent experience).
  • 2+ years of back-end development experience.

Responsibilities:

  • Design, implement, and maintain server-side application logic.
  • Develop and optimize database schemas and queries.
  • Build and consume RESTful APIs and microservices.
  • Ensure application scalability, performance, and security.
  • Collaborate with front-end developers to integrate user-facing elements.

Full-Stack Developer

Requirements:

  • Strong proficiency in both front-end (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) and back-end (Node.js, Python, or Java) technologies.
  • Experience with front-end frameworks (React, Angular, or Vue.js) and back-end frameworks (Express, Django, Spring, etc.).
  • Database experience (SQL & NoSQL).
  • Familiarity with RESTful APIs, GraphQL, or microservices.
  • Understanding of DevOps practices (CI/CD, Docker, Kubernetes).
  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or equivalent experience.
  • 3+ years of full-stack development experience.

Responsibilities:

  • Architect and develop both client-side and server-side components.
  • Implement database designs and optimize queries.
  • Build and consume APIs (RESTful/GraphQL).
  • Ensure application performance, scalability, and security end-to-end.
  • Collaborate with UI/UX designers to translate mockups into production code.
  • Maintain and refactor existing codebase across the full stack.
